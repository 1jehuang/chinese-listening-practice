CHIN 111 Test 1 Review

First-year Chinese for Heritage Learners - Autumn 2025

📋 Test Format

Oral Test (30%)

  • Pinyin minimal pairs (without tones)
  • Pinyin with tones (Four tones and Neutral tone)
  • Chinese Characters

Expectations:

  • Read pinyin and characters correctly & without ambiguity
  • Complete within 2.5 minutes

Written Test (70%)

  • Dictation for Pinyin
    • Circle the correct pinyin
    • Fill in the blank with correct initial, final, tone mark
    • Write down the pinyin you hear
  • Chinese Characters

🎵 Tones

Tone Mark Placement

Rule: Follow the sequence: a o e i u ü

Special Rule: For -iu, tone mark is placed on u

diū xiū qiú liù

Neutral Tone (轻声)

Example: 小心地滑 vs 小心的滑

xiǎo xīn dì huá xiǎo xīn de huá

🔤 Initials - Minimal Pairs

Common Minimal Pairs to Practice:

da-ta ni-li shi-xi zi-qi ca-qia lai-lei tui-dui nong-long ke-he sun-xun chuan-quan xian-qian zhen-zeng shang-xiang

z/c/s vs j/q/x

zī - jī cī - qī sī - xī zá - jiá cá - qiá sá - xiá zūn - jūn cūn - qūn sūn - xūn

📝 Finals

Key Distinctions:

in vs ing

yīn - yīng mín - bīng qīn - qíng pīn - mìng

en vs eng, an vs ang

zhēn - zhèng rèn - zhèng zēng - zōng dōng - zhòng

un vs ün, uan vs üan

zūn - jūn cūn - qūn sūn - xūn zuān - juān cuān - quān suān - xuān

ou vs iu

zhōu - xiū jiǔ - zhōu qiū - shōu
