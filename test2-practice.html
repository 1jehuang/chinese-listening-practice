📚 8 Grammar Patterns to Master

1

A跟B一样( + adj.) / A比B adj.

Pattern: Comparison structures (same as / more than)

Examples:

  • 我跟你一样高。(I'm as tall as you.)
  • 他比我高。(He's taller than me.)
  • 这个跟那个一样好。(This one is as good as that one.)

Practice: Fill in the blank

我的房间___你的房间一样大。

Answer: 跟 (gēn)

2

V1 着 V2

Pattern: Simultaneous actions (doing V1 while V2)

Examples:

  • 我坐着看书。(I sit while reading.)
  • 他站着说话。(He stands while talking.)
  • 她笑着回答。(She answers while smiling.)

Practice: Translate to Chinese

He eats while watching TV.

Answer: 他看着电视吃饭。

3

才1: Time + 才 + verb

Pattern: Late occurrence / later than expected

Examples:

  • 他十点才来。(He didn't come until 10 o'clock.)
  • 我昨天才知道。(I only found out yesterday.)
  • 她今天才到。(She only arrived today.)

Practice: Complete the sentence

我等了很久，他十一点___到。

Answer: 才 (cái)

4

才2: (A 不verb), B 才 verb

Pattern: Contrastive emphasis (only when/if B happens)

Examples:

  • 你不说，我才不知道。(If you don't say it, I won't know.)
  • 他不来，我才不去。(If he doesn't come, I won't go.)

Practice: Translate

If you don't help, I won't do it.

Answer: 你不帮，我才不做。

5

到底

Pattern: Emphatic "actually/really/in the end" in questions

Examples:

  • 你到底去不去？(Are you going or not?!)
  • 他到底是谁？(Who is he really?)
  • 这到底是什么？(What is this actually?)

Practice: Make the question more emphatic

你住在哪儿？→ 你___住在哪儿？

Answer: 到底 (dàodǐ)

6

看起来 / 看出来

Pattern: Appearance (看起来) vs. Perception (看出来)

Examples:

  • 他看起来很高兴。(He looks happy.)
  • 我看出来他不高兴。(I can tell he's not happy.)
  • 这个菜看起来很好吃。(This dish looks delicious.)

Practice: Choose 看起来 or 看出来

她___很累。(She looks tired.)

Answer: 看起来 (kàn qǐlái) - describes appearance

7

QW + 都

Pattern: Universal quantifier (every/all)

Examples:

  • 谁都喜欢他。(Everyone likes him.)
  • 哪儿都有人。(There are people everywhere.)
  • 什么都好。(Everything is fine.)
  • 我什么都吃。(I eat everything.)

Practice: Translate to Chinese

Everyone knows this.

Answer: 谁都知道这个。(Shéi dōu zhīdào zhège.)

8

只要…就…

Pattern: Conditional "as long as A, then B"

Examples:

  • 只要你来，我就高兴。(As long as you come, I'll be happy.)
  • 只要天气好，我就去。(As long as the weather is good, I'll go.)
  • 只要有时间，我就学习。(As long as I have time, I'll study.)

Practice: Complete the sentence

___你努力，___会成功。(As long as you work hard, you'll succeed.)

Answer: 只要 (zhǐyào) ... 就 (jiù) ...

✍️ Translation Practice

1. Chinese → English

我的宿舍跟你的一样大。

Answer: My dorm room is as big as yours.

2. English → Chinese

He didn't arrive until 9 o'clock.

Answer: 他九点才到。(Tā jiǔdiǎn cái dào.)

3. Chinese → English

你到底住在哪儿？

Answer: Where do you actually live? / Where do you live anyway?

4. English → Chinese

As long as you study hard, you will succeed.

Answer: 只要你努力学习，就会成功。(Zhǐyào nǐ nǔlì xuéxí, jiù huì chénggōng.)

5. Chinese → English

我坐着看书。

Answer: I sit while reading. / I read while sitting.

📝 Writing Prompts

Prompt 1: 我的宿舍 (My Dorm Room)

Write 4-6 sentences describing your dorm. Use at least 2 of the grammar patterns.

Suggested patterns: 跟...一样, V1着V2, 看起来

Prompt 2: 我的一天 (My Day)

Describe your daily routine. Use at least 3 grammar patterns.

Suggested patterns: 时间+才+verb, V1着V2, 只要...就...

Prompt 3: 比较 (Comparison)

Compare two things (people, places, objects). Use comparison patterns.

Required patterns: A跟B一样, A比B, 看起来
